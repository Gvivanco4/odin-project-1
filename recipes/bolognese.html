Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti bolognese consists of spaghetti (long strings of pasta) with an Italian rag√π (meat sauce) made with minced beef, bacon and tomatoes, served with Parmesan cheese.
Spaghetti bolognese is one of the most popular pasta dishes eaten outside of Italy.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Sugar
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Red Wine
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Saute garlic and onion about 3 minutes;
  2. Brown the beef about 2 minutes;
  3. Add everything else, give it a good stir then simmer for 20 minutes minimum (midweek), up to 3 hours (weekend indulgence!).
    Slow cooking makes the beef incredibly tender and the sauce develops extra flavour.
