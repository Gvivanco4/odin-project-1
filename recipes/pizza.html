Pizza recipe

Description

Pizza is one of the most popular dishes in Italy. It has been globalized by its delicious flavor
Making and impact regardless the country. And the best part? It's easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Peperoni

Steps

  1. Spread the bread
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Spread the sauce in the bread
  4. Spread the cheese around the circle
  5. Put the peperoni
  6. Bake for 50 min
